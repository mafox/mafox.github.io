Notes on Biology. Organelles and Lysosomes.

Organelles: Organelles are the functional and structural components of a cell. Examples of organelles include the nucleus, mitochondria, lysosomes, Golgi apparatus, endoplasmic reticulum, and chloroplasts. These organelles have specialized functions that help the cell to survive and perform its functions.Lysosomes are small organelles that contain digestive enzymes that break down and recycle cellular waste,such as proteins and old cell parts. They are also involved in the digestion of ingested food particlesand the destruction of invading microorganisms.
